System FAQ

Will this wrinkle my clothes?

Because the air is removed evenly, clothes are pressed flat, not crumpled. We recommend rolling garments before compressing for the best wrinkle-free results.

What if I don't have a pump when traveling back?

The VacPack features a dual-valve system. If you lose the included mini-pump, you can simply sit on the bag to force the air out of the one-way manual release valve.

Does it have space for my laptop?

Yes. The rear panel features an independent, padded sleeve designed for 16-inch laptops. This section is isolated from the vacuum chamber to protect your tech from compression.
